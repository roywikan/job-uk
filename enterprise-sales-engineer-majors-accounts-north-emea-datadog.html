Datadog - Enterprise Sales Engineer, Majors Accounts, North EMEA - Sweden

Company: Datadog

Location: Sweden

Job Type: Full-time

Salary:

Job Description

As a Sales Engineer supporting Major Accounts, you will work hand in hand with your sales counterpart to foster success and growth in our largest, most strategic accounts. As a critical part of the account team, SEs use their technical expertise to strategize with customers on their observability strategy, conduct product demonstrations, and support technical evaluations. This is an influential ro...le, where Sales Engineers help qualify and close opportunities with customers and have a voice with the product team to help prioritize features based on input from customers, competitors, and partners.

At Datadog, we place value in our office culture - the relationships and collaboration it builds and the creativity it brings to the table. We operate as a hybrid workplace to ensure our Datadogs can create a work-life harmony that best fits them.

What You’ll Do:
• Partner with the Sales team, Technical Account Managers, CSMs, and others on the broader account team to articulate the overall Datadog value proposition, vision and strategy to customers
• Own technical engagement with customers as new teams evaluate the platform and guide existing teams in evaluating new products. Communicate Datadog’s value based on activities and work with customers on any identified issues or concerns to successful conclusion
• Technically close complex opportunities through advanced competitive knowledge, technical skill, and credibility
• Deliver product and technical briefings / presentations and articulate the successes achieved through Datadog platform adoption in Quarterly Business Reviews.
• Maintain accurate notes and feedback in CRM regarding customer input both wins and losses
• Proactively engage and communicate with customers and Datadog business/technical teams regarding product feedback and competitive landscape

Who You Are:
• Passionate about educating customers on observability risks that are meaningful to their business, and able to build and execute an evaluation plan with a customer
• Collaborative partner who works side by side with sales to creatively engage with customers. Together you’ll investigate current state, build relationships with customers to understand their needs and priorities with the goal of offering a prescriptive observability strategy that delivers on their needs.
• Someone with strong written and oral communication skills. This role requires an ability to understand and articulate both the business benefits (value proposition) and technical advantages of our offering
• Experienced in programming/scripting with any of the following: Java, Python, Ruby, Go, Node.JS, PHP, and .NET etc.
• Someone with a minimum of 3+ years in a Sales Engineering or DevOps Engineering role
• Able to sit up to 4 hours, traveling to and from client sites
• Able to travel via auto, train or air up to 45% of the time

Datadog values people from all walks of life. We understand not everyone will meet all the above qualifications on day one. That's okay. If you’re passionate about technology and want to grow your skills, we encourage you to apply.

Benefits and Growth:

We Are Group - Freelance Digital Skills Trainer - Remote - England, United Kingdom

Job Description

Role: Freelance Digital Skills Trainer - Remote Pay rate: £20ph Location: Remote About We Are Group:We Are Group is a Social Impact Company dedicated to delivering positive outcomes for our service users. Our mission is to empower individuals to improve their lives and future potential by providing access to digital and online services. We believe in bridging the digital divide helping indiv iduals access services, products, and advice online, one click at a time. By introducing people to the digital world, we enable them to unlock a more secure and fulfilling future. Social impact is at the core of what we do, so much so that it’s written into our charter: if there’s no impact, we don’t start the work. We merge education and technology to create opportunities and drive positive change in communities. When individuals unlock life chances, they bring value to the broader community. What Our Trainers Do:Our trainers make a tangible difference in the lives of people across the UK. They provide vital support to individuals facing everyday digital-related challenges, helping them access the online world with confidence. This year, we’re expanding our efforts to support members of the public who need assistance with their compensation claims through the Windrush Compensation Scheme. Trainers will provide guidance, complete online forms, and offer digital upskilling tailored to their needs. The work our trainers do transforms lives—empowering learners with the confidence and skills to navigate digital spaces independently. About the Role of a Digital Skills Trainer:This is a subcontracted, fully remote role with a competitive rate of £20 per hour. Trainers will assist members of the public with various digital skills, including:• Windrush Scheme Support: Assisting individuals with their compensation claims by completing online forms and providing related guidance.• Assisted Digital: Helping learners complete online forms and access critical services.• Digital Inclusion: Training learners to improve their digital skills and navigate online platforms.• Ensuring that learners feel confident and empowered to continue using digital services independently. About You:We’re looking for individuals who are passionate about making a difference and possess the following qualities:• Experience working with vulnerable adults through local authorities, government programs, or volunteer roles (preferred)• Proficiency in using Microsoft Office, internet, and other digital platforms.• High-level communication skills, both verbal and written.• Patience and an understanding approach to meeting diverse learner needs.• BPSS clearance or the ability to obtain it is essential. If you’re motivated to create positive social impact and have the skills to empower others through digital education, apply now to become a Digital Skills Trainer!

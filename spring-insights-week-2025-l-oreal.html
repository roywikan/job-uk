L‘OREAL - Spring Insights Week 2025 - United Kingdom

Company: L‘OREAL

Location: United Kingdom

Job Type: Full-time

Salary: £450–£500 a day

Job Description

When you think of L'Oréal, do you see more than just lipstick? We invite you to look deeper and experience the freedom to go beyond the ordinary during our exclusive Spring Virtual Insights Week. For over a century, L'Oréal has been a global leader in beauty, driven by innovation, scientific excellence, and a commitment to creating beauty that moves the world. With a portfolio of 37 global brands sold in 135 markets, L'Oréal offers a diverse range of career opportunities. This exclusive virtual program will provide:Inside L'Oréal: Explore our diverse business operations, discover potential career paths, and learn about the day-to-day experiences of our interns and management trainees. Gain insights into different departments and functions, from marketing and research to supply chain and digital innovation. Skill-Building Workshops: Enhance your employability with workshops focused on assessment centre success and CV writing techniques, equipping you with the tools to excel in your job search. L'Oréal's Commitments: Discover L'Oréal's commitment to Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DE&I), and learn how these principles are embedded within our company culture and business practices. Explore our ambitious sustainability program, "L'Oréal for the Future," and our vision for a more sustainable beauty industry. Networking Opportunities: Connect with L'Oréal professionals, ask questions, and build valuable relationships within our global network. Who Should Attend? This program is ideal for current first-year university students or those who have not yet earned a degree and are:• Passionate about the beauty industry and curious about L'Oréal.• Eager to learn about an entrepreneurial business and expand their professional network.• Considering applying for future L'Oréal internships or graduate schemes. Application Process:• Eligibility Check: Click "Apply" to answer a few questions confirming your eligibility.• Digital Assessment: Eligible candidates will receive an email with a link to a digital assessment, comprising:• Written Responses: Answer three open-ended questions. (Not timed)• Personality Traits: Complete a personality assessment.• Cognitive Test: Engage in short-term memory games and abstract/logical reasoning exercises.• Review and Outcome: Successful candidates will be contacted by a recruiter via email. Ready to experience L'Oréal? Apply Now!
